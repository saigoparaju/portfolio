The food and beverage culture are the main cuisine of Bahamas. They often include the dishes like fish, Shellfish, Lobster, Crab , conch and other seafood. Bahamas is also famous for tropical fruits, rice, peas, pigeons, potatoes and pork. Some famous seasonal dishes include chilies, lime, cilantro, tomatoes, oninos, garlic, allspice, cinnamon and coconut. The most of famous beverages are Rum based.The food culture has been changing since time. Urban residents typical meal consists of fruits, vegetables, meat, fish bread and rice.


















Staples of Bahamas are Sea food. Conch is served raw in lime juice and spice or deep-fried. The steamed conch is used in soups. The clawless spiny lobster and crabs are pretty famous. The fresh fish includes grouper is served fried.

The famous Bahamas Beverage is coconut water blended with sweet milk and gin. Switcha is made with native lime.Some of the beverages are Fruit Juices,Coconut water, Switcha,Goombay Punch

The Bahamas famous Desserts are Tarts, Duff,Bread pudding, Rum cake and Cormeal pudding. Tarts are made of fish with corn powder mixed with special curry powders. The bread pudding is a bread based dessert with milk, egg, sugar, dried fruits as ingredients.

The Alton's Lentil soup,Salmon vegetable chowder, Ginger Carrot soup, Pesto Soups are famous Bahamas soups.The Alton's Lentil soup is the most famous, special, delicious and unique as it is made of special spices grown and processed natively
